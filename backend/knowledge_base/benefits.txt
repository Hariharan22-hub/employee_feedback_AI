Employee Benefits

All full-time employees are eligible for health insurance covering the employee and up to 4 dependents.
The company offers a retirement savings plan with a 6% employer match on employee contributions up to 8% of base salary.
Wellness benefit: employees receive an annual wellness stipend of $500 for gym memberships, mental health apps, or fitness equipment.
Employee Assistance Program (EAP) provides free, confidential counseling sessions (up to 6 per year) for employees and immediate family.
Remote work equipment stipend: $300 one-time allowance for home office setup for eligible remote employees.
