Company Rules and Code of Conduct

Employees are expected to treat colleagues with respect and professionalism at all times.
Harassment, discrimination, or bullying of any kind will not be tolerated and should be reported immediately to HR or via the anonymous ethics hotline.
All harassment reports are investigated confidentially within 5 business days of being filed.
Conflicts of interest, including outside employment that competes with company business, must be disclosed to HR.
Company property, including laptops and access credentials, must be returned upon termination of employment.
Violations of the code of conduct may result in disciplinary action up to and including termination.
