Compensation and Pay Policy

Salaries are reviewed annually during the March performance cycle, with adjustments effective April 1.
Performance bonuses are calculated based on individual rating (50%) and company performance (50%), paid out in Q1 of the following year.
Overtime for non-exempt employees is paid at 1.5x the standard hourly rate for hours worked beyond 40 per week.
Pay equity reviews are conducted twice a year by the Compensation & Benefits team to identify and correct unjustified pay gaps.
Employees may request a compensation review through their manager or directly through the HR portal.
