Employee Handbook - General Policies

Standard working hours are 9:00 AM to 6:00 PM, Monday through Friday, with flexible start times between 8:00-10:00 AM.
Remote work: employees may work remotely up to 3 days per week, subject to manager approval and role requirements.
Probation period for new hires is 90 days, during which performance and fit are formally reviewed.
Employees may request internal transfers after completing 12 months in their current role, subject to manager and HR approval.
Career development: each employee has an annual training budget of $1,000 for courses, certifications, or conferences.
