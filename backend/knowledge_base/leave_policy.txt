Leave Policy

Employees are entitled to 24 days of paid annual leave per calendar year, accrued monthly at 2 days per month.
Sick leave: up to 12 days per year with a medical certificate required for absences longer than 2 consecutive days.
Maternity leave: 26 weeks of paid leave for the primary caregiver.
Paternity leave: 4 weeks of paid leave.
Leave requests must be submitted at least 5 working days in advance through the HR portal, except for sick leave.
Unused annual leave: up to 10 days may be carried over into the next calendar year; any excess is forfeited.
